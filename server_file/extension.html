Overview

Quantify the environmental impacts of ChatGPT usage including carbon, water, and energy consumption.

Estimate the environmental footprint of your ChatGPT usage in real time! Using the length of a generated response and your coarse location, AI Impact Tracker quantifies the carbon, water, and energy usage associated with your activity.

Click here to install!

Key Features:

  • Estimates environmental impacts by measuring output tokens (similar to word count) in generated responses.
  • Combines token data with your coarse location (by country, state or province, and watershed ID) to model localized grid and water resource impacts.
  • Collected data is securely analyzed at aiimpacttracker.cs.haverford.edu so you can view aggregated, community-wide environmental impacts.

Privacy-First Design:

  • The extension never stores the actual content of your prompts or responses (just the length of the responses).
  • We strictly limit collection to the number of output tokens, your coarse location, and the date. Everything else remains completely private.
